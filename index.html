MERRICK

On February 11 of 1993, Merrick was born in New York City at Mt. Sinai Hospital. Merrick grew up in the small town of Annandale, New Jersey. At the age of 13, Merrick began writing lyrics. He began recording songs throughout high school, and has since started performing his songs in and around Miami, he goes to the U. Current projects include Elite 8, as well as Space Jams and future projects which are in the works. More to come.
